Matchmaker

Hello, this is a fun little application that will help me see if you and I are compatible. The instructions are pretty easy to understand,
all you have to do is read the 5 following questions and choose your response in the drop down following each question. The answers
are fairly self-explanatory, strongly agree all the way down to strongly disagree. After you have answered all questions you will click the
"Calculate Compatibility" button at the bottom andout will come your compatibility score. Good Luck!!!
